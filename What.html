What is renewable energy?

Renewable energy is energy that can be acuired from natrual sources that can be
compensated for in a humans lifetime unlike nonrenewable energy where the sources
that are used for it need hundreds of years to compensate for its usage. There are
multiple forms of renewable energy and that what these website is going to talk about!
