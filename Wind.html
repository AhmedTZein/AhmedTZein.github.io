Wind Energy

Wind energy is one of the most common forms of renewable energy. Wind turbines use
large blades that are uniquely shaped to be pushed by the air to gyrate and create
mechanical energy that is used to drive a turbine that converts the mechanical
energy into electrical energy. Wind turbines are a great way of harnessing wind
energy because although it requires a lot of energy to be constructed it can make
the same amount of energy back within 6 months and can still continue operating for
25 years.
